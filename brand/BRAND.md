# Heirloom — Brand Book

> The definitive brand constitution, built from zero on **The Deep**. This file supersedes
> every prior design language — the legacy "austere literary vault" (Cormorant, monastic
> 70%-empty, bare infinity glyph, glowing filament web) AND the interim "Held" cloth/copper/ink
> system (Warp-and-Weft mark, ember-on-ink) are both retired and are **not** a starting point.
> What follows is binding. When code and this file disagree, fix the code.
>
> The only fixed truths above the brand are the **product truth** (Heirloom is a perpetual,
> append-only, multi-author, multi-generational family Thread, web at `heirloom.blue` + PWA)
> and the **frozen commercial facts** (Free $0 · Family $6.99/mo · $69/yr · Founder $249
> role-only, withdrawn from sale · ZAR localization untouchable). Everything visual and
> verbal in this book is ours to hold.

## 0 — Why we started over

The domain is `heirloom.blue`. The one thing every prior team kept and loved is the **water** —
the dye-seeded living surface behind the app. Yet the brand on top of it was cloth, copper, and
ink: a different material fighting both the name and the backdrop. A true blank page honours what
is already true. The name is blue. The surface is water. So the brand is **The Deep**.

A family runs deep. What you lower into deep water stays, and can be drawn back up by anyone who
comes after. That is exactly what Heirloom is: an append-only record where nothing is removed,
where the past sits quietly below the present, and where a descendant a hundred years from now can
sound the depth and bring a voice back to the surface. The metaphor is not decoration — it **is**
the data model (append-only, multi-generational, never deleted) made visible.

## 1 — Essence

**Depth.**

Not depth as difficulty. Depth as water: what you lower in stays, and rises again when someone
reaches for it. Heirloom is the deep water a family keeps — voices going down across generations,
the present a warm line at the surface, everything below it held and recoverable.

That one word, *depth*, is the whole company. It is what a grandmother feels when she lowers in a
story knowing it will outlast her, and what a grandchild feels years later when it surfaces in
their hands. It is the opposite of *stored*, *backed up*, *archived in the cold sense*. We do not
warehouse data. We hold a family's voices the way still water holds the sky.

## 2 — Promise

> **You keep lowering things in. We keep them — held at depth, drawn back up to the right person
> at the right moment, written, spoken, and passed on, for as long as your family lasts.**

Both halves are load-bearing. The family does the keeping — they write, they speak, they pass on.
We do the holding — safe, deep, reaching the person you meant. We never claim to *be* the memory.
We are the water, not the voice.

## 3 — Positioning & Tagline

**Master hero line — use this first, everywhere it counts:**

> ## Some things only get deeper.

It is the most emotional and the least boastful line we have. It assumes the reader already knows
what those things are — a marriage, a grief, a family. It never says *forever*, never says
*legacy*, never raises its voice. It is true of water and true of love.

**Supporting line — used after the hero, never as the hero:**

> A perpetual family archive — owned by a bloodline, not a login.

**Durability proof-point — a single quiet line, never a headline:**

> Built to outlast everyone who uses it.

The in-market phrase *"Start your family's thousand-year thread"* is **demoted to a durability
proof-point** — it may appear once, low and quiet, as evidence of permanence. It is referenced by
the commercial vocabulary, so it is not deleted. But it boasts, so it is never the first thing
anyone reads.

**The positioning, in one breath:** Heirloom is the deep water a family keeps the conversation in
across generations — written, spoken, and passed on at exactly the right moment — held safe for as
long as the family lasts.

## 4 — Personality & Voice

The voice of someone alive at the kitchen table at golden hour. Warm, present-tense, second
person, **addressed to a real named person**. Concrete and sensory — hands, weather, food, a
kitchen, a name. Occasionally funny, occasionally aching, never bleak, never saccharine.
Plainspoken and certain without being clinical. Reverent at the sealed and release moments, never
funereal. Premium but unintimidating: a 34-year-old new parent and their 78-year-old grandmother
both find it beautiful and both find it easy.

We say **keep**, **lower in**, and **pass on**. We never say *death*, *legacy data*, or
*memorialize*. The water metaphor stays under the surface of the copy — we let the interface carry
it, and we never strain a sentence to force in *deep* or *sink*.

### The seven voice rules

1. **Lead every sensitive moment with arrival-of-love, never loss.** *"For Maya, when she turns
   30"* — not *"after you're gone."* Death is the mechanism in the architecture; it is never the
   foreground emotion and never in acquisition copy.
2. **Present tense, second person, name a real person.** *"Tell your daughter what the kitchen
   smelled like"* beats *"preserve a memory."* Sensory and concrete over abstract.
3. **Use the kept-vocabulary** (§9). *Keep, lower in, pass on, held until, opens when, choose your
   thread.* Banish *memorialize, vault, secure your legacy, before it's too late.*
4. **No urgency, ever.** No countdowns, scarcity, streak guilt, FOMO, A/B pressure — and
   absolutely none near sealing, verification, release, or pricing. The thousand-year promise dies
   the instant the product behaves like quarterly-growth SaaS.
5. **No gamification of memory.** No badges, points, completion %, leaderboards. Reinforcement is
   relational and warm: *"Maya read your kitchen story,"* never *"+1, 3-day streak."*
6. **The verification speaks as calm caretaking.** *"A quiet note — just confirming all is well.
   No action needed if it is. Take your time."* Infrequent, reversible, reverent.
7. **AI is a labelled midwife, never a ventriloquist.** Gentle prompts, narration, dye suggestions
   — clearly marked as a tool. Never generate words or a voice impersonating a real (especially
   deceased) person.

### Do / Don't — example lines

| Surface | Say this | Never this |
|---|---|---|
| Hero | Some things only get deeper. | Secure your legacy before it's too late. |
| The Listener (daily) | What did your mother always say in the car? | You haven't written in 5 days. |
| Sealing a note | Held until Maya turns 30. | Locked. Releases in 4,015 days. ⏳ |
| Release (after passing) | Someone left this for you. It opens when you're ready. | Deceased member's memorial vault is now unlocked. |
| Verification (DMS) | A quiet note — just confirming all is well. No action needed if it is. | Confirm you are alive within 7 days or your account will be released. |
| Reinforcement | Your grandmother read your kitchen story. | +10 pts! 3-day streak! 🔥 |
| Social CTA | Ask someone tonight what their childhood bedroom smelled like. | Sign up now — limited spots! |
| Pricing | Family — $6.99/mo. Everyone in. | Upgrade now or lose your memories! |
| Onboarding | Choose your thread. | Pick your profile colour. |
| AI assist | A prompt to help you start — your words, always. | We wrote this memory in your grandfather's voice. |

## 5 — Distinctive Asset System

Seven assets do the recognition work. No icon library, no decorative emoji.

1. **The Sounding mark.** Concentric depth-rings with one warm surface-line — the act of sounding
   the deep, going down and bringing back. It is ownable because it is the product's own data model
   — a deep, append-only record with a single living present at the surface — made into a glyph.
   (Full law in §8.)
2. **Your colour for life.** At joining, each member chooses one of the ten named natural dyes. It
   becomes their name colour, their 3px left margin-thread everywhere forever, **and a tint in the
   family's water**. The onboarding ritual is literally *"choosing your thread."* Nobody else has
   this memory hook.
3. **The Family Spectrum.** The exact dyes a specific family contains — a coat of arms generated
   from real data, and the literal seed of that family's water colour. It heads their Book cover
   (foil bands), their share cards, and the anniversary email footer. Every family's brand is
   literally theirs alone.
4. **Dyes named, not numbered.** *Madder, cochineal, kermes, saffron, weld, walnut, oakgall, woad,
   indigo, iron* — pigments with centuries of meaning instead of hex codes. A verbal and chromatic
   asset at once.
5. **The Listener.** One quiet daily prompt — the signature content unit across product, social,
   and email. Also a call-and-response engine that surfaces a real family member's past entry to
   answer across time: *"Your grandmother wrote about this kitchen in 1979 — what do you remember?"*
6. **The mono archival stamp.** Dates, generation counts, keeper lineage, thread IDs — set in
   uppercase tracked mono. The same *"this is a record that outlives you"* watermark from the
   website footer to the printed Book colophon.
7. **The reverent release lexicon.** A fixed, gentle vocabulary set in the display serif — *held
   until, opens when, for Maya when she turns 30, passed on to you by.* Dignity rendered as a
   repeatable, designed asset. No countdowns, locks, or skulls.

## 6 — Visual System

Three honest materials: **deep water, dyed light, a warm surface-line.**

### 6.1 Space — the descent grid

Layout sits on an **8px base rhythm** (4px sub-grid). Each content thread hangs off a **left margin
line** — the member's 3px dye thread, reading as their own line descending into the family's depth.
A single reading column, **max 66ch measure** for prose. Body type is **≥17px everywhere** —
multigenerational legibility is non-negotiable.

Negative space is structure, but **inhabited**: target **~55–60% empty** — calm, deep, and
tactile, never the old monastic 70%-empty unreadable vault. The living **water is the home plane**;
UI sits on a quiet **surface plane** a few millimetres above it, with generous margins so the depth
shows through.

Material references stay abstract and premium — real still-water and depth-of-field light, a
genuine rise/sink motion language. **Never** literal droplet clip-art, cartoon waves, rubber-duck
whimsy, glassmorphism, conic-gradient mesh, animated noise, glowing filament webs, or AI "organic
blob" art.

### 6.2 Type

Two variable serifs, one sans, one mono. The type stack is deliberately **kept from the prior
system** — these faces were already right for an heirloom, and The Deep needs new colour and a new
mark, not new letterforms. Cormorant remains **dropped entirely** — it is the dated vault tell.

| Role | Family | Settings | Where |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Display / Voice** | **Fraunces** (variable) | `opsz` ~110–120 at hero · weight ~360 · `SOFT` 0 · `WONK` 0 · tracking −0.01em | Hero lines, the Listener prompt, sealed/release copy, chapter openers. Never below its ~24px optical floor. Never running body. |
| **Reading / Prose / Inputs** | **Source Serif 4** (variable) | 17px / 1.6 | The memory-body workhorse — so a hundred-year-old letter reads like a letter, not an app. |
| **UI chrome** | **Inter** (variable) | tall x-height, calm | Buttons, nav, form labels, small product UI. |
| **Archival / Metadata** | **JetBrains Mono** | UPPERCASE · +0.12em tracking · tabular numerals | Dates, generation counts, keepership roles, entry IDs, the colophon, the OG footer stamp. Used sparingly. |

Type scale is fluid (clamp-based) — ~1.5 ratio at display, ~1.2 in body.

**Wordmark:** *Heirloom* in Fraunces, `opsz` ~110, weight ~360, all lowercase except a capital `H`,
tracking −0.01em. The two adjacent `o`s may optionally render as two faint concentric rings echoing
the Sounding mark — a quiet, never-required flourish.

### 6.5 The hierarchy rule

**Gold = NOW** (the live present, the surface-line, the call to write). **Dye = WHO** (the family
member, their thread, their tint in the water). **Blue depth = the medium and the kept past** —
the further down, the older and quieter; past and sealed content sits deep and dim, never gold.
This single rule governs product, email, social, and Book. If you are unsure whether something
earns gold, it does not.

## 7 — Motion

One calm spring, physics-based — **stiffness ~170, damping ~26** — for entrances and layout.
Micro-states reduce to a **220ms ease**. **Things settle; they never bounce.** Durations cluster at
**180 / 360 / 720 / 1400ms**.

Every motion carries information, and the metaphor is **rise and sink**:

- A new entry or member **surfaces** — rises from depth to the present line.
- A note **sinks** as it seals — lowered below the surface, the water closing quietly over it.
- Past entries rest **at their depth** (older = deeper, cooler, dimmer); scrolling back is
  descending.
- On load, the **gold surface-line draws once across** (~1400ms), then rests on the living water.

**Nothing loops forever** in the UI chrome. The water itself moves perpetually and untouched (it is
kept exactly as-is) — but UI gestures breathe once and stop.

**The Seal gesture** is the one moment of deliberate ceremony: a slow **press-and-hold / lower**
that feels like letting something down into still water — gravity in the hand, not in a warning
banner. No gold urgency, no countdown.

`prefers-reduced-motion` is **fully honoured** — sections appear without rising; content renders at
rest. No spinners anywhere; loading is the hairline **ProgressHair**. No toasts; status is inline.

## 8 — Mark Usage Law

The **Sounding mark**: concentric depth-rings crossed by one warm surface-line — sounding the deep.
Never a glowing node, a filament web, or a literal cartoon droplet. Master geometry (viewBox
`0 0 192 192`):

- **Ground:** solid `deep #070d14`, full bleed (master). Transparent variant permitted for overlay.
- **Five concentric rings**, centred `(96, 96)`, in `bone #f2e6d0`, round-cap stroke 3px, at radii
  `r = 18, 34, 50, 66, 80`. Alpha **descends outward** — innermost ring ~0.92, then ~0.72, ~0.50,
  ~0.32, ~0.18 — so the well visibly fades into depth.
- **One horizontal surface-line** across the full mark at `y = 96`, `x = 16 → 176`, in
  `surface #e0a062`, round-cap stroke 4px, with a 1.5px `surface-bright #f0c074` core. The line
  passes **over** every ring. This is the **only** warm colour and the **only** emphasis in the
  mark — the single living present laid across the kept depth.

**Favicon** (viewBox `0 0 48 48`): simplified so it reads at 16px — three rings (`r = 6, 12, 18`,
stroke 1.6px, bone, descending alpha) and the gold surface-line at `y = 24` (`x = 4 → 44`, stroke
2px) passing over them. Reads as a sounding into water, never a generic target or `◎`.

**Maskable tile:** `deep` bleeds to the edge; the rings + surface-line live inside the inner 80%
safe circle, geometrically centred so the gold line crosses tile centre and survives the iOS
squircle and Android circle masks.

**Reversed** (on shallows): rings in `indigo #3a5582`, surface-line gold unchanged — for the light
theme and the Book cover deboss.

**Lockup:** *Heirloom* in Fraunces (`opsz` ~110, weight ~360, lowercase but capital `H`, tracking
−0.01em). Horizontal lockup = mark tile at left, mark height = cap-height of the `H`, optical gap
≈ 0.6× cap-height between mark and wordmark.

### Mark do-not list
- No glowing radial node, converging filament web, "cosmic loom," or sci-fi mark.
- No literal cartoon water droplet, splash, or wave clip-art.
- Never recolour the rings to a dye, or the surface-line to anything but gold. The mark is
  bone-on-deep (or indigo-on-shallows) + the one gold line, always.
- Never add a second warm element, gradient mesh, bevel, or drop shadow. The descending ring-alpha
  is the only depth cue.
- Never stretch, rotate, or outline the mark.
- Never substitute the bare `∞` or any icon-library glyph.

Shipped assets: `brand/mark/heirloom-sounding-192.svg`, `brand/mark/heirloom-sounding-favicon-48.svg`,
`brand/mark/heirloom-sounding-maskable-512.svg`, `brand/mark/heirloom-sounding-lockup-shallows.svg`,

## 10 — Sensitive Surfaces (handle with dignity)

- **Sealing** is rendered as a note **lowered into still water** with a mono date label — the
  surface closing quietly over it. Never a padlock, hourglass, or countdown. The ceremony is the
  press-and-hold gesture; the dignity is the fixed reverent copy.
- **The dead-man's-switch / verification** speaks as calm caretaking, is infrequent, and is always
  reversible: *"A quiet note — just confirming all is well. No action needed if it is. Take your
  time."*
- **The after-passing release** is the most carefully made object in the system: reverent, no
  marketing, no app chrome, **no gold urgency**. The recipient controls **when** they open it.
  *"Someone left this for you. It opens when you're ready."*
- **Pricing** is stated plainly and never pressured: Free $0 · Family $6.99/mo or $69/yr · Founder
  $249 (role only, withdrawn from sale). ZAR localization is untouchable. No A/B pressure on price,
  ever.

## 11 — The Absolute Do-Not List

1. No countdown timers, scarcity, streaks, FOMO, or urgency anywhere — categorically forbidden near
   sealing, the dead-man's-switch, release, and pricing.
2. Never lead with death, loss, tombstones, hourglasses, padlocks, "deceased," "memorialize," or
   "before it's too late." Death lives in the architecture, never the foreground.
3. No glowing radial node, converging filament web, sci-fi "cosmic loom" mark, or literal cartoon
   droplet/splash.
4. No glassmorphism, frosted/blurred translucency, conic-gradient mesh, animated noise, 3D bevels,
   or AI "organic blob" shapes. **The water backdrop is the sole living surface and is kept exactly
   as-is — never re-skin, blur, or layer effects over it.**
5. No Cormorant Garamond; never set running body in the display serif or drop it below ~24px.
6. No dye colours as fills, buttons, or solid backgrounds — signal only (3px margin-thread + name
   text + water tint).
7. No icon libraries or decorative emoji; the mark and the mono stamp carry recognition.
8. No avatar circles / `rounded-full` chips, no loading spinners (use ProgressHair), no toasts
   (inline status only).
9. No gamification of memory — no badges, points, completion %, leaderboards, or read-receipts
   framed as performance.
10. No performance pressure at the Composer — no audience counters, no AI quality-scoring, no
    "great memories get more reads."
11. No AI ventriloquism — never generate words or a voice impersonating a real or deceased person.
12. Never A/B-pressure or alter the frozen pricing; ZAR localization is untouchable.
13. No stock photography in email; no marketing chrome or gold urgency on the after-passing release.
14. Never reintroduce the austere 70%-empty monastic vault, the cloth/copper "Held" system, or tiny
    unreadable type.
